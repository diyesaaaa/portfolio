DinoRush

Pygame - Python


Dino Rush is a modern version of the Chrome Dino game, featuring a simplistic side-scrolling endless runner concept. Developed using pygame, a collection of Python modules that works across various platforms, the game aims to give life to the original black and white Chrome Dinosaur Game.

Dino Rush maintains a user-friendly approach, offering players the option to select from three distinct worlds: the cave, forest, and city. To commence the game, players need to press the "spacebar," prompting the dinosaur character to begin running. Players have control over the dinosaur's actions by using specific keys on their keyboard. The spacebar allows them to make the dinosaur jump, while pressing the arrow up allows them to make the dinosaur duck. By pressing the spacebar, the dinosaur leaps, allowing it to evade a variety of obstacles that emerge on the screen. The longer players can ensure the dinosaur's survival without encountering obstacles, the higher their score will climb.
